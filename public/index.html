Welcome to Mariner's Pathway

Mariner's Pathway is dedicated to guiding aspiring mariners on their journey to a rewarding career at sea. Our programs provide the skills, knowledge, and support needed to succeed in the maritime industry.

Deck Officer Courses

Deck Officer Class I

The highest level of deck officer certification, qualifying you to serve as Master (Captain) on merchant vessels of any size and tonnage worldwide. This course covers advanced ship management, leadership, navigation, and maritime law.

Deck Officer Class II

Prepares you for the Chief Mate examination, focusing on cargo operations, ship stability, advanced navigation, and management duties.

Deck Officer Class III

The Officer of the Watch (OOW) certification for merchant shipping. This course provides the essential knowledge and skills for safe navigation, watchkeeping, and operational duties as a junior deck officer.
