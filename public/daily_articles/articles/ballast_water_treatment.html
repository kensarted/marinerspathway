Ballast Water Treatment Systems

Step 1: Filtration

  • Remove large portion of microorganisms

Step 2

  • One of the following:

    • Chemical Disinfection

      • Chlorine: Inactivate microorganism
      • Before final deballasting â€“ to be neutralized or detoxified

    • U.V Treatment

      • Ultraviolet lamps: Impact DNA of organism and render non-viable
      • Can be affected by water with low Total Suspended Solid

    • Deoxygenation

      • Inert gas/Nitrogen: To asphyxiate the organisms
      • Takes 2 to 4 days and tanks to be sealed, not recommended for short transits

    • Heat Treatment

      • Heating: Kills organism in BW
      • Takes long time, can cause greater corrosion in tank

    • Ultrasonic/Cavitation Treatment

      • High energy ultrasound: To eliminate organism
      • Break down organism cells and killing them
